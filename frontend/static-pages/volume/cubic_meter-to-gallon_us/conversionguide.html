The Core Conversion

The cubic meter (m³) is the standard unit of volume in the International System of Units (SI), while the US gallon is commonly used in the United States for liquid measurements. Converting between these units allows precise volume calculations.

1 m³ = 264.172 US gallons

Example:

0.01 m³ → 0.01 × 264.172 ≈ 2.64 US gallons

The Core Conversion

Use this formula for reverse conversion:

m³ = US gallons ÷ 264.172

Example:

5 US gallons → 5 ÷ 264.172 ≈ 0.0189 m³
