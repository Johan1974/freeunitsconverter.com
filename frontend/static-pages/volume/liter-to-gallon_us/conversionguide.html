Liters to Gallons Converter

Converting liters to gallons accurately is essential for cooking, science, shipping, and everyday measurements. The liter is the standard metric unit for volume, while gallons come in two major types: U.S. gallons and Imperial gallons. Our liters to gallons converter makes it easy to switch between these systems instantly and accurately.

Core Conversion Formula

The conversion depends on the type of gallon you are using:

  • U.S. Gallons: 1 liter = 0.264172 U.S. gallons
  • Imperial Gallons: 1 liter = 0.219969 Imperial gallons

To convert liters to gallons, multiply the number of liters by the appropriate conversion factor:

Example: 5 liters × 0.264172 = 1.32086 U.S. gallons

Reverse Conversion — Gallons to Liters

Converting gallons back to liters is just as straightforward:

  • Liters = U.S. gallons ÷ 0.264172
  • Liters = Imperial gallons ÷ 0.219969
Example: 2 U.S. gallons ÷ 0.264172 ≈ 7.57 liters
