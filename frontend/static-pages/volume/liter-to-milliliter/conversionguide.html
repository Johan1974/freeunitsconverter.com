Liter to Milliliter Converter

Convert volumes between liters (L) and milliliters (mL) quickly and accurately. Our Liter to Milliliter converter is ideal for cooking, scientific experiments, medical measurements, and industrial applications.

The Core Conversion

The liter is a widely used metric unit of volume, while the milliliter is a smaller metric unit, often used for more precise measurements. Accurate conversion ensures correct volume interpretation.

1 Liter = 1,000 Milliliters

Example:

2.5 Liters → 2.5 × 1,000 = 2,500 mL

Milliliter to Liter Converter

Sometimes you need to convert milliliters back to liters. Our Milliliter to Liter converter allows fast and precise reverse conversions for recipes, lab work, or industrial needs.

The Core Conversion

Use this formula for reverse conversion:

Liters = Milliliters ÷ 1,000

Example:

750 mL → 750 ÷ 1,000 = 0.75 L
