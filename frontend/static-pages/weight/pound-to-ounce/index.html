The Core Conversion: Pounds to Ounces

A pound is a standard imperial unit of weight, primarily used in the United States, while an ounce is a smaller unit within the same system. To convert pounds to ounces, multiply the number of pounds by 16, as there are 16 ounces in one pound.

oz = lb × 16

Example:

5 lb → 5 × 16 = 80 oz

Ounce to Pound Converter

Sometimes you need to convert back to pounds. Our Ounce to Pound converter provides fast and accurate results for recipes, shipping calculations, or other weight conversions.

The Core Conversion

Since 1 pound equals 16 ounces, you can convert ounces to pounds using this formula:

lb = oz ÷ 16

Example:

80 oz → 80 ÷ 16 = 5 lb
