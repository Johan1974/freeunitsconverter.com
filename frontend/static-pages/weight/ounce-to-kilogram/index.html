Convert weights between ounces (oz) and kilograms (kg) quickly and accurately with our Ounce to Kilogram converter. This converter is useful for cooking, shipping, science experiments, and fitness, especially when handling smaller weights that are commonly measured in ounces.

The Core Conversion: Ounces to Kilograms

The ounce is a unit of mass primarily used in the United States, while the kilogram is the standard metric unit used worldwide. To convert ounces to kilograms, use the following formula:

kg = oz × 0.0283495

Example:

16 oz → 16 × 0.0283495 ≈ 0.4536 kg

The Core Conversion

Since 1 kilogram equals approximately 35.274 ounces, the conversion formula is:

oz = kg × 35.274

Example:

0.5 kg → 0.5 × 35.274 ≈ 17.637 oz
