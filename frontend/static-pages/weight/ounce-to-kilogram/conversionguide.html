The Core Conversion: Ounces to Kilograms

The ounce is a unit of mass primarily used in the United States, while the kilogram is the standard metric unit used worldwide. To convert ounces to kilograms, use the following formula:

kg = oz × 0.0283495

Example:

16 oz → 16 × 0.0283495 ≈ 0.4536 kg
