The Core Conversion: Ounces to Pounds

An ounce is a smaller unit of mass commonly used in the United States, while a pound represents a larger standard weight unit in the imperial system. To convert ounces to pounds, divide the number of ounces by 16 because there are 16 ounces in a pound.

lb = oz ÷ 16

Example:

32 oz → 32 ÷ 16 = 2 lb

The Core Conversion

Since 1 pound equals exactly 16 ounces, use this formula for accurate conversions:

oz = lb × 16

Example:

3.5 lb → 3.5 × 16 = 56 oz
