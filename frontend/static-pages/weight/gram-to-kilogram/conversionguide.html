The Core Conversion

Grams and kilograms are both metric units. 1 kilogram equals 1,000 grams. Converting ensures accurate weight interpretation in all contexts.

kg = g ÷ 1000

Example:

500 g → 500 ÷ 1000 = 0.5 kg

The Core Conversion

g = kg × 1000
