The Core Conversion: Kilograms to Pounds

The kilogram is the standard metric unit for mass, used worldwide. The pound is an imperial unit commonly used in the United States and the UK. To convert kilograms to pounds, use this formula:

lb = kg × 2.20462

Example:

5 kg → 5 × 2.20462 ≈ 11.023 lb
