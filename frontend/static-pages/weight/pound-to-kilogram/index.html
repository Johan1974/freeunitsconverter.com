The Core Conversion: Pounds to Kilograms

A pound is an imperial unit of weight, primarily used in the United States, while a kilogram is the standard metric unit used internationally. To convert pounds to kilograms, divide the number of pounds by 2.20462.

kg = lb ÷ 2.20462

Example:

50 lb → 50 ÷ 2.20462 ≈ 22.68 kg

The Core Conversion

Since 1 kilogram equals approximately 2.20462 pounds, use this formula for accurate conversions:

lb = kg × 2.20462

Example:

22.68 kg → 22.68 × 2.20462 ≈ 50 lb
