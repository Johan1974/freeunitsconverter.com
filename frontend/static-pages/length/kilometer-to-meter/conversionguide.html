The Core Conversion Formula

The kilometer (km) is a unit in the metric system, representing 1,000 meters (m). The conversion is straightforward:

1 kilometer = 1,000 meters

To convert kilometers to meters manually, simply multiply the number of kilometers by 1,000. For example:

3 km × 1,000 = 3,000 m

Reverse Conversion: Meters to Kilometers

Converting meters back to kilometers is equally simple:

Kilometers = Meters ÷ 1,000

3,000 m ÷ 1,000 = 3 km
