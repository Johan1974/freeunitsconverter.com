The Core Conversion Formula

A centimeter (cm) is a metric unit of length, whereas an inch (in) belongs to the imperial system, primarily used in the United States. The standard conversion is:

1 centimeter = 0.393701 inches

To convert centimeters to inches manually, multiply the number of centimeters by 0.393701. For example:

10 cm × 0.393701 = 3.93701 in

Reverse Conversion: Inches to Centimeters

If you have measurements in inches and need to convert them back to centimeters, use:

Centimeters = Inches ÷ 0.393701

3.93701 in ÷ 0.393701 = 10 cm
