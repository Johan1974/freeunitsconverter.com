The Core Conversion Formula

The foot (ft) is an imperial unit of length, while the millimeter (mm) belongs to the metric system. The standard conversion is:

1 foot = 304.8 millimeters

To convert manually, multiply the number of feet by 304.8. For example:

5 ft × 304.8 = 1,524 mm

Reverse Conversion: Millimeters to Feet

If you have measurements in millimeters and want to convert them back to feet, the formula is:

Feet = Millimeters ÷ 304.8

1,524 mm ÷ 304.8 = 5 ft
