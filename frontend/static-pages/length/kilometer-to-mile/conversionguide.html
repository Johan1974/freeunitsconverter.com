The Core Conversion

The kilometer (km) is the base unit of length in the metric system, recognized worldwide. The mile (mi) belongs to the imperial system and is used primarily in the U.S. Knowing how to use a reliable kilometers to miles converter is essential.

1 kilometer ≈ 0.621371 miles

To convert manually, multiply the number of kilometers by 0.621371. For example:

5 km × 0.621371 ≈ 3.106855 mi

Miles to Kilometers

You can convert back from miles to kilometers using the reverse formula:

Kilometers = Miles ÷ 0.621371

3.106855 mi ÷ 0.621371 ≈ 5 km
