Convert Foot to Inches Online Accurately

Converting feet to inches is a common requirement in construction, woodworking, interior design, and personal measurements. Whether you want to convert foot to inches online or manually, precise conversion ensures accuracy in all projects.

The Core Conversion Formula

The foot (ft) is an imperial unit of length, and the inch (in) is also part of the imperial system. The standard conversion is:

1 foot = 12 inches

To convert manually, multiply the number of feet by 12. For example:

5 ft × 12 = 60 in

Reverse Conversion: Inches to Feet

If you have measurements in inches and need to convert back to feet, the formula is simple:

Feet = Inches ÷ 12

60 in ÷ 12 = 5 ft

Tips for Accurate Conversion

1. Always multiply by 12 to convert feet to inches.
