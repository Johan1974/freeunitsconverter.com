Convert Meters to Feet Accurately Online

Converting meters to feet is essential for construction, engineering, travel, and personal measurements. Whether you need to convert meters to feet online or manually, knowing the exact conversion ensures precision in all your projects.

The Core Conversion Formula

The meter (m) is the base unit of length in the metric system, while the foot (ft) is used in the imperial system. The standard conversion is:

1 meter = 3.28084 feet

To convert meters to feet manually, multiply the number of meters by 3.28084. For example:

5 m × 3.28084 = 16.4042 ft

Reverse Conversion: Feet to Meters

You may also need to convert feet back to meters. The formula is:

Meters = Feet ÷ 3.28084

16.4042 ft ÷ 3.28084 = 5 m
