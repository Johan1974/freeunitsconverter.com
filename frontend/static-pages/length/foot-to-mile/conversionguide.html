The Core Conversion Formula

The foot (ft) is an imperial unit, and the mile (mi) is commonly used in the United States and the UK for measuring long distances. The standard conversion is:

1 foot = 0.000189394 miles

To convert manually, multiply the number of feet by 0.000189394. For example:

5,280 ft × 0.000189394 = 1 mi

Reverse Conversion: Miles to Feet

If you have distances in miles and want to convert them back to feet, the formula is:

Feet = Miles ÷ 0.000189394

1 mi ÷ 0.000189394 = 5,280 ft
