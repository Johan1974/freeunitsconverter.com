Convert Inches to Feet Accurately Online

Converting inches to feet is a common requirement in construction, carpentry, engineering, and daily measurements. Whether you need to convert inches to feet online or manually, knowing the exact conversion ensures precision in all your projects.

The Core Conversion Formula

The inch (in) is a unit of length in the imperial system, while the foot (ft) is also used in imperial measurements. The standard conversion is:

1 foot = 12 inches

To convert inches to feet manually, divide the number of inches by 12. For example:

24 in ÷ 12 = 2 ft

Reverse Conversion: Feet to Inches

You may also need to convert feet back to inches. The formula is simple:

Inches = Feet × 12

2 ft × 12 = 24 in

Why Use an Online Inches-to-Feet Converter?
