Converting centimeters to millimeters is essential for precision measurements in engineering, science, crafts, and everyday tasks. Whether you need to convert centimeters to millimeters online or manually, understanding the conversion ensures accuracy.

The Core Conversion Formula

A centimeter (cm) is part of the metric system, and a millimeter (mm) is a smaller metric unit. The standard conversion is:

1 centimeter = 10 millimeters

To convert manually, multiply the number of centimeters by 10. For example:

5 cm × 10 = 50 mm

Reverse Conversion: Millimeters to Centimeters

To convert millimeters back to centimeters, divide the number of millimeters by 10:

Centimeters = Millimeters ÷ 10

50 mm ÷ 10 = 5 cm
