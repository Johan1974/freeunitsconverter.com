Convert Meters to Feet Online Accurately

Converting meters to feet can be essential for construction, engineering, travel, or personal measurements. Whether you need to convert meters to feet online or manually, understanding the exact conversion ensures accuracy in all situations.

The Core Conversion Formula

The meter (m) is the base unit of length in the metric system, while the foot (ft) is used in the imperial system, primarily in the United States. The standard conversion is:

1 meter = 3.28084 feet

To convert meters to feet manually, simply multiply the number of meters by 3.28084. For example:

5 m × 3.28084 = 16.4042 ft

Reverse Conversion: Feet to Meters

Sometimes you may have measurements in feet and need to convert them back to meters. The formula is simple:

Meters = Feet ÷ 3.28084

16.4042 ft ÷ 3.28084 = 5 m
