The Core Conversion Formula

The mile (mi) is an imperial unit of length primarily used in the United States, while the yard (yd) is also part of the imperial system. The standard conversion is:

1 mile = 1,760 yards

To convert miles to yards manually, multiply the number of miles by 1,760. For example:

2 mi × 1,760 = 3,520 yd

Reverse Conversion: Yards to Miles

If you have a distance in yards and want to convert it back to miles, the formula is:

Miles = Yards ÷ 1,760

3,520 yd ÷ 1,760 = 2 mi
