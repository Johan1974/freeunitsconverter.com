Converting feet to yards is important in construction, sports, and everyday measurements. Whether you need to convert foot to yard online or manually, using the correct conversion factor ensures precision in all situations.

The Core Conversion Formula

Both the foot (ft) and yard (yd) are units in the imperial system. The standard conversion is:

1 yard = 3 feet

To convert feet to yards manually, divide the number of feet by 3. For example:

9 ft ÷ 3 = 3 yd

Reverse Conversion: Yards to Feet

To convert yards back to feet, multiply the number of yards by 3:

3 yd × 3 = 9 ft
