The Core Conversion Formula

The meter (m) is the base unit in the metric system, and the inch (in) is a standard unit in the imperial system. The conversion is:

1 meter = 39.3701 inches

To convert manually, multiply the number of meters by 39.3701. For example:

2 m × 39.3701 = 78.7402 in

Reverse Conversion: Inches to Meters

To convert inches back to meters, use the formula:

Meters = Inches ÷ 39.3701

78.7402 in ÷ 39.3701 = 2 m
