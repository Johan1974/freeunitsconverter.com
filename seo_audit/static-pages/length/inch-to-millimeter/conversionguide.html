Convert Inches to Millimeters Online Accurately

Converting inches to millimeters is important in engineering, manufacturing, construction, and DIY projects. Whether you need to convert inches to millimeters online or manually, understanding the exact conversion ensures precision for all applications.

The Core Conversion Formula

The inch (in) is an imperial unit, while the millimeter (mm) belongs to the metric system. The standard conversion is:

1 inch = 25.4 millimeters

To convert inches to millimeters manually, multiply the number of inches by 25.4. For example:

5 in × 25.4 = 127 mm

Reverse Conversion: Millimeters to Inches

To convert millimeters back to inches, the formula is:

Inches = Millimeters ÷ 25.4

127 mm ÷ 25.4 ≈ 5 in
