The Core Conversion Formula

The inch (in) is part of the imperial system, while the yard (yd) is another imperial unit commonly used in the United States. The standard conversion is:

1 yard = 36 inches

To convert inches to yards manually, divide the number of inches by 36. For example:

72 in ÷ 36 = 2 yd

Reverse Conversion: Yards to Inches

Converting yards back to inches is simple:

Inches = Yards × 36

2 yd × 36 = 72 in
