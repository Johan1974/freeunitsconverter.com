The Core Conversion Formula

The meter (m) is the base unit of length in the metric system, and the millimeter (mm) is a subunit used for smaller measurements. The standard conversion is:

1 meter = 1,000 millimeters

To convert meters to millimeters manually, multiply the number of meters by 1,000. For example:

5 m × 1,000 = 5,000 mm

Reverse Conversion: Millimeters to Meters

If you have a measurement in millimeters and want to convert it back to meters, use:

Meters = Millimeters ÷ 1,000

5,000 mm ÷ 1,000 = 5 m
