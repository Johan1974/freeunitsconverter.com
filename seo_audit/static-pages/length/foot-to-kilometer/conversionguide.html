Convert Foot to Kilometers Online Accurately

Converting feet to kilometers is often necessary in engineering, surveying, travel planning, and scientific calculations. Whether you want to convert foot to kilometers online or manually, understanding the precise conversion ensures accurate results for any project.

The Core Conversion Formula

The foot (ft) is an imperial unit of length, while the kilometer (km) is part of the metric system. The standard conversion is:

1 foot = 0.0003048 kilometers

To convert manually, multiply the number of feet by 0.0003048. For example:

5,000 ft × 0.0003048 = 1.524 km

Reverse Conversion: Kilometers to Feet

If you have measurements in kilometers and need to convert back to feet, the formula is:

Feet = Kilometers ÷ 0.0003048

1.524 km ÷ 0.0003048 = 5,000 ft
