The Core Conversion: Grams to Ounces

The gram is a metric unit of mass, widely used internationally, while the ounce is an imperial unit primarily used in the United States. To convert grams to ounces accurately, use the following formula:

oz = g ÷ 28.3495

Example:

100 g → 100 ÷ 28.3495 ≈ 3.53 oz

The Core Conversion

Since 1 ounce is equal to 28.3495 grams, you can convert ounces to grams using this formula:

g = oz × 28.3495

Example:

5 oz → 5 × 28.3495 ≈ 141.75 g
