The Core Conversion: Grams to Pounds

The gram is a metric unit of mass, widely used internationally, while the pound is an imperial unit commonly used in the United States and the UK. To convert grams to pounds accurately, use the following formula:

lb = g ÷ 453.59237

Example:

1000 g → 1000 ÷ 453.59237 ≈ 2.20462 lb

The Core Conversion

Since 1 pound is equal to 453.59237 grams, you can convert pounds to grams using this formula:

g = lb × 453.59237

Example:

5 lb → 5 × 453.59237 ≈ 2267.96 g
