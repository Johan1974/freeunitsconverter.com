The Core Conversion: Kilograms to Ounces

The kilogram is the standard metric unit for mass, used worldwide. The ounce is an imperial unit commonly used in the United States and the UK. To convert kilograms to ounces, use this formula:

oz = kg × 35.27396

Example:

2 kg → 2 × 35.27396 ≈ 70.55 oz
