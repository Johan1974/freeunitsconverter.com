US Gallon to Liter Converter

Convert volumes between US gallons (gal) and liters (L) quickly and accurately. Our US Gallon to Liter converter is perfect for cooking, automotive, scientific, and industrial applications.

The Core Conversion

The US gallon is a customary unit of volume used primarily in the United States, while the liter is the standard metric unit for liquid volumes internationally. Accurate conversion ensures precise measurement across different systems.

1 US Gallon = 3.78541 Liters

Example:

2 US gallons → 2 × 3.78541 ≈ 7.57082 L

Liter to US Gallon Converter

Sometimes you need to convert liters back to US gallons. Our Liter to US Gallon converter ensures you can reverse the calculation accurately for recipes, automotive needs, or scientific work.

The Core Conversion

Use this formula for reverse conversion:

US Gallons = Liters ÷ 3.78541

Example:

10 liters → 10 ÷ 3.78541 ≈ 2.6417 US gallons
