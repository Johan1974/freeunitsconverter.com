US Gallon to Milliliter Converter

The Core Conversion

The US gallon is a customary unit of volume mainly used in the United States, while the milliliter is a metric unit used worldwide. Accurate conversion is crucial for proper measurement across different systems.

1 US Gallon = 3,785.41 Milliliters

Example:

1.5 US gallons → 1.5 × 3,785.41 ≈ 5,678.12 mL

Milliliter to US Gallon Converter

Sometimes you need to convert milliliters back to US gallons. Our Milliliter to US Gallon converter allows fast and precise reverse conversions for recipes, lab work, or industrial needs.

The Core Conversion

Use this formula for reverse conversion:

US Gallons = Milliliters ÷ 3,785.41

Example:

10,000 mL → 10,000 ÷ 3,785.41 ≈ 2.6417 US gallons
